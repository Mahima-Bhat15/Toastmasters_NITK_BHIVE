Who Are We?

Chartered on 29th February 2020, our club has over 20+ members. This club is founded by NITK Surathkal alumni and the weekly meetings are held at BHIVE workspace. Hence the club name is NITK BHIVE Toastmasters Club. Most of the members are Rotarians who share the ideals of service, friendship, diversity, integrity, and leadership. This club is a part of District 92 Division B Area 1.

Where do we meet?
